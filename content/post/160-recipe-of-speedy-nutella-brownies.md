##### Steps to make Nutella Brownies:

1. Preheat oven to 325° F
1. Grease an 8 inch baking pan
1. Combine flour and salt in bowl and set aside
1. Beat eggs, nutella, brown sugar, and vanilla together until smooth.
1. Alternate adding flour and butter mixing each time until smooth. If dry, an additional 1/4 c melted butter can be added.
1. Scrape batter into greased pan.
1. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until knife comes out mostly clean (few crumbs).
1. allow to cool completely, cut and serve.
