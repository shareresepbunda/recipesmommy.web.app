##### Instructions to make Spring frittata:

1. Spray a lasagna glass dish with the oil, lay the potato slices and sprinkle with lemon salt and Provence herbs.
1. Top the potatos with the onions, than with the asparagus, than with the endives.
1. Sprinkle with fresh ground black pepper and oregano. Top with crumbled feta cheese.
1. Wisk the eggs with the milk and the flour in a small bowl. Pour over the veggies.
1. Cover with foil, pierce foil so steam can come out and cook in a pre-heated oven at 375° for 30 minutes.
1. Uncover and continue cooking for another 15 minutes. Finish at broil for a couple of minutes.
1. Serve with a drizzle of maple sirup (optional).
1. Enjoy!
