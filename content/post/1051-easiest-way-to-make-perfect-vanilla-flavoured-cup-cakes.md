1. Add 2 tbsp oil in it.
1. Pour 2 cups milk.
1. 2 tbsp vanilla essence.

1. Mix all dry and liquid ingredients well.
1. Now grease the tea cups with oil.
1. Pour 3 tbsp mixture in all cups and bake cup cakes on 180° for 35 minutes.
