##### The ingredients needed to make Sour Cream &amp; Onion Crusted Pork Chops:

1. Prepare 1 bag of sour cream &amp; onion chips
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Make ready 1 family pack of assorted pork chops




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sour Cream &amp; Onion Crusted Pork Chops:

1. Clean chops and set to the side; preheat oven at 375°F.
1. Using a gallon size Ziploc bag, combine chips and seasoning. Use either a mallet or rolling pin to crush chips.
1. Place each chops in bag to make sure it&#39;s covered in both sides. Place on baking sheet lined with foil or on wire baking rack.
1. Place chops in oven and cook for 30-35 min.
1. You can plate it with your favorite pasta. Enjoy!!!!
