##### Steps to make Caramel Banana Tarte Tatin:

1. In an oven-safe 23cm pan, make a caramel using the sugar and a squeeze of lemon, sprinkle sea salt.
1. Slice 3 bananas, place them in the caramel, cut a sheet of ready made puff pastry in the shape of the pan, cover the pan with the pastry and bake at 200C for 25mins.
1. Cool for 10mins, upend onto a plate. Serve with ice cream and chopped hazelnuts.
