##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Garlic &amp; Chorizo Barley Risotto:

1. Take 1 bulb garlic
1. Make ready 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
1. Prepare 1 x3 inch piece of Spanish chorizo
1. Make ready 3/4 cup pearl barley, rinsed
1. Take 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
1. Get 2 generous cups of good chicken stock
1. Prepare 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

##### Instructions to make Roasted Garlic &amp; Chorizo Barley Risotto:

1. Heat the oven to 200 C/400 F
1. Place the garlic bulb on a piece of kitchen foil (if you are doing more than one, do them seperately)
1. Pour the teaspoon of oil over the garlic bulb.
1. Wrap the bulb up in the foil like a package, twisted at the top to make it easy to open.
1. Place the garlic in an oven tray and put in the oven to roast for 30 minutes.
1. Remove garlic from the oven to cool a little while you start on the risotto.
1. Remove any casing from around the chorizo, and chop into half inch cubes.
1. Put a pot over a medium low heat and add the chorizo. Stir it until it starts to sizzle and the oils are coming out of it.
1. Add the chopped rosemary and the pearl barley, and stir it for around 30 seconds.
1. Add the stock and bring it up to the boil.
1. Lower heat and simmer over a medium heat for 30 minutes, or until the stock is almost all absorbed. You can stir it every now and then, but it isn&#39;t necessary to stand over it like rice.
1. Meanwhile, open up the foil, snip the top of the garlic bulb, and open it up. Peel or squeeze the garlic cloves and put them in a pestle and mortar or a food processor. Mash or whiz it up until smooth.
1. If you have a smell of garlic on your fingers  use a steel soap with cold water, or rub your hands and fingers on a stainless steel faucet with cold water until the smell is gone.
1. Season the risotto, then stir in the garlic &#39;butter&#39; and serve hot.
