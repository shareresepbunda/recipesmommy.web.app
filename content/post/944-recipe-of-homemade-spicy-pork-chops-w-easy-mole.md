##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Pork Chops w/ Easy Mole:

1. Take 4 pork chops
1. Take 1 bottle Dona Maria mole paste
1. Make ready 1/2 cup honey
1. Prepare 1 cup water
1. Take 1 tbsp ground red pepper
1. Take 2 1/2 tsp ground coriander
1. Prepare 2 tsp garlic powder
1. Make ready 2 tsp onion powder
1. Take 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
1. Take 1/3 tsp cinnamon
1. Make ready 1 large pinch salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Spicy Pork Chops w/ Easy Mole:

1. Heat mole paste, water, cinnamon, and honey is a small sauce pan. Whisk.
1. Toss chops with enough oil to coat. Season. Sear in a large oven-safe saute pan. Sear one side and flip over. After very briefly searing the other side, pour mole over the chops and finish in a 350° oven for approximately 10 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness.
