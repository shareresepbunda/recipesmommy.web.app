##### The ingredients needed to make Salsa:

1. Make ready 1/2 Small Onion

1. Prepare 3 tbsp cilantro
1. Get 1 Chipotle in adobo sauce (not the whole can)
1. Get  salt
1. Get 1/2 of a lime (juice)
1. Get 1/4 cup water
